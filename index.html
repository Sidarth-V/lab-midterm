WELCOME TO GOA

Seafood at Zeebop

Goa provides a languid and relaxed atmosphere, particularly in comparison with the north of the country, and for me this began as soon as I met other members of The Goa Experience team at Zeebop, a famous beach shack located on Utorda Beach. After a couple of weeks of non-stop curry, it was wonderful to enjoy some of Goa’s famous seafood.

Arossim Beach sunset

Before landing in Goa, I spent two weeks backpacking in the north of India, exploring the Golden Triangle and the desert cities of Rajasthan. The north was incredible; a chaotic and mesmerising mass of multisensory experiences, but I was certainly ready for a more laid-back impression of India as I boarded my flight to Goa.


The Latin Quarter

The region is dominated by Goa’s capital city, Panjim, and we were able to spend an hour or two here walking around the beautiful Latin Quarter where one of our new properties, The Panjim Inn, is located. The hustle and bustle of Central Goa continues in immediate North Goa, with beach resorts such as Candolim, Calangute and Baga providing more vibrancy than South Goa.
